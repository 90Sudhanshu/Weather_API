Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction. AI is used in a wide range of fields including healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment.

Types of AI

  • Reactive Machines
  • Limited Memory
  • Theory of Mind
  • Self-Aware

Applications of AI

  1. Speech Recognition
  2. Image Recognition
  3. Natural Language Processing
  4. Robotics
  5. Autonomous Vehicles
